What is Medi Predict?

Medi Predict is an innovative AI-powered chatbot designed to transform the way you manage your health. By simply inputting your symptoms, Medi Predict can predict potential diseases and provide you with a percentage likelihood of each possible condition. This tool leverages advanced algorithms and a vast database of medical knowledge to offer you quick, accurate, and personalized health insights.
